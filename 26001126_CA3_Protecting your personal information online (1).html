Protecting Your Personal Information Online

Personal information includes data like your address, phone number, and login credentials. Always think twice before sharing any of these details online.

Quick Tips:

  • Use strong passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Dont click on unknown links

Steps to Secure Your Info:

  1. Check website security (HTTPS)
  2. Use antivirus software
  3. Update your software regularly

Key Terms:

Phishing
A scam where attackers trick you into revealing personal info.
Encryption
Protects your data by converting it into unreadable code.
